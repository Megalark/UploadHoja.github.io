FAQ

In this case, not so frequently.

Is the software free?

Synthesis is a completely free and open source video editing software. Anyone on the internet can download it. However, resale or selling the software under your name is prohibited.

What are the minimum requirements for Synthesis?

The software is made in such a manner that almost all home computers will be able to run it. Any CPU which comes after the fifth generation and 4gb of RAM should be enough to power it.

Why is the installer stuck?

The installer may be stuck due to over heating of the device or some minor bug in the launcher. Try relaunching the installer. If the problem still persists, kindly contact us.

Will Synthesis be coming to mobile platforms?

Currently, we don't have any such plans to launch Synthesis on mobile platforms, however if the demand is high we may consider it.

Are the previous versions available?

Since Synthesis is relatively new, the previous versions won't be available for download as they are buggy. In the coming future however, we will be uploading the previous versions of the software.

How frequent are the updates?

We like to keep our customers as satisfied as we can and thus we update the software as frquently as possible. New features come with each update so check them out!
